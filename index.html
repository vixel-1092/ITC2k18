About the college

The college has a well established infrastructure with 162 experienced faculty members. Among them 40 are with Ph.D and 51 are pursuing Ph.D. Research and Development is given more importance in the college. The total research grant received from funding agencies like AICTE, DST, DRDO, MNRE, MSME and ISRO is Rs. 487.45 Lakhs. The college has excelled in academics consistently. It has produced 45 University Ranks in UG (2012-16) and 13 University Ranks in PG (2014-16 batch). Placement is also given due importance in the college. 37.60 % of the eligible students got placed in leading companies namely ZOHO, Juspay, CODA Global,Sirius Technologies, Maveric Systems, Amazon, WIPRO, embedUR Systems, Amphisoft, Elitists, Smacar,Urjanet, VVDN, Infoview, Solartis, Saint Gobain, Visual BI, MBIT Wireless, Sundaram Clayton, Infosys, Sify Technologies, Pfizer (ICU), GSS, Verizon, Paypal, Byjus, Neeyamo, PositiveNaick, ZoomRx, Virtusa, Karomi Technologies, DirectI, Alight solutions, eLitmus, Torry Harris, Abyeti, CTS, Anarock, HPE, ClusterX, Sanmar, Wheels India in the current academic year till 27th Dec.2017. Students who have achieved both in academics and sports are to be honoured during the college day. Rs. 6,37,220/- lakhs worth prizes are won by students in 2016-17 and they are awarded with an equal prize money during the College day. The students who stand first in academics are given cash prizes. Faculty who produced centum results are appreciated with 2000/- Rs cash award for each paper & 1000/- for more than 95%. The total prize amount received by the faculty members was Rs 7,32,200/- lakhs in the academic year 2016-17.

About the Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering is a centre of excellence for computer science teaching and research. It was established in 2007 and it started with 60 students and 4 faculty members. Now it has grown to 477 students and 26 faculty members. The department offers both Under Graduate and Post Graduate courses in CSE. The department has well equipped computer lab with centralized monitoring facility and it is an approved Research Centre of Anna University, Chennai. The Department has signed MoUs with CICSO, TYCO Electronics and Pearson VUE authorized testing center and has received funds from DST, DRDO , MNRE, ISRO and IEI worthy of Rs.111.96 Lakhs for R & D work.

About the Conference

About the Conference: The proposed conference is with an objective to provide opportunity to the academicians, researchers and industry representatives to present their work in the identified areas of engineering. The interactions among the presenters, juries and audience will help to strengthen the technology innovation and to formulate solutions to the challenges of the mankind. The main areas of deliberations are related to the current trends in engineering domains related to computing, electronics and communication, information systems control and automated systems. The researchers will be able to identify the theme and subareas like distributed computing. cloud computing, algorithms, big data analytics, Embedded systems, web technology, Communication System.
